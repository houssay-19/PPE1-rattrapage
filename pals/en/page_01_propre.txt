sunlight provides energy that green plants use to create sugars
mostly form starches which release energy into living
things that digest them this process photosynthesis provides
virtually all energy used by living things some species animals
generate their own light a process called bioluminescence for
example fireflies use light to locate mates and vampire squid use it
to hide themselves from prey
